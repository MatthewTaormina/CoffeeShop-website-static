Coffee

At Coffee Heaven, we take pride in our Canada-first approach—sourcing premium beans from ethical Canadian roasters and highlighting local flavors in every cup. Our menu features a wide variety of expertly crafted coffee drinks, from rich espressos and velvety lattes to our signature Maple Latte and the beloved Double Double Classic. We believe in celebrating Canadian traditions, which is why our top items include the True North Espresso and Canuck Cappuccino, each inspired by the spirit and taste of Canada. Whether you’re craving a classic brew or a uniquely Canadian creation, you’ll find something to love in our thoughtfully curated selection.
